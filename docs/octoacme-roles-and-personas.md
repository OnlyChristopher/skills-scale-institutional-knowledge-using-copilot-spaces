# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Process Owner

### Role Summary
Process Owners oversee the integrity and continuous improvement of project management processes. They ensure process documentation is accurate, standards are upheld, and the team follows established best practices.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain and continuously improve project management processes
- Ensure process documentation is accurate and up-to-date
- Monitor adherence to established standards and procedures
- Coordinate with project managers to gather feedback on processes
- Facilitate process evolution based on team needs and lessons learned
- Provide process training and guidance to team members

### Goals
- Drive consistent execution through well-defined processes
- Improve project efficiency through process optimization
- Reduce process-related confusion and bottlenecks
- Ensure processes scale with organizational growth

### Typical Communication
- Monthly process review meetings with project managers
- Process update notifications and training sessions
- Documentation of process changes and improvements
- Feedback collection through surveys and retrospectives

---

## Quality Assurance Lead

### Role Summary
Quality Assurance Leads define and monitor project quality standards throughout the development lifecycle. They work with project managers and team leads to integrate quality checkpoints and ensure deliverables meet defined criteria.

### Responsibilities
- Define quality standards and acceptance criteria
- Establish QA checkpoints throughout project phases
- Monitor quality metrics and test coverage
- Collaborate with developers on testing strategies
- Review deliverables for quality compliance
- Identify quality risks and recommend mitigations

### Goals
- Maintain high quality standards across all deliverables
- Reduce defects and production issues
- Improve test coverage and automation
- Build a culture of quality within the team

### Typical Communication
- Quality gate reviews at major milestones
- Weekly quality metrics reports
- Test plan reviews and test case documentation
- Bug triage meetings and defect reports

---

## Stakeholder Liaison

### Role Summary
Stakeholder Liaisons serve as the main point of contact between the project team and key stakeholders. They communicate updates, gather feedback, and ensure requirements are accurately captured and understood.

### Responsibilities
- Manage stakeholder relationships and expectations
- Communicate project updates and status to stakeholders
- Gather and document stakeholder feedback and requirements
- Ensure stakeholder concerns are addressed promptly
- Facilitate stakeholder meetings and presentations
- Translate business needs into actionable project requirements

### Goals
- Maintain strong stakeholder engagement and satisfaction
- Ensure clear, timely communication of project status
- Minimize misunderstandings about requirements
- Build trust between the project team and stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly stakeholder status updates
- Bi-weekly stakeholder review meetings
- Requirement clarification sessions
- Escalation communications when issues arise

---

## Change Manager

### Role Summary
Change Managers coordinate and document process or scope changes, ensuring proper communication and risk assessment. They facilitate the change approval workflow and maintain accurate documentation of all changes.

### Responsibilities
- Coordinate the change management process
- Document and track all change requests
- Assess impact and risks of proposed changes
- Facilitate change approval workflow
- Communicate approved changes to all affected parties
- Update project documentation to reflect changes
- Maintain change logs and audit trails

### Goals
- Ensure controlled and transparent change processes
- Minimize disruption from changes to scope or process
- Maintain project stability while enabling necessary flexibility
- Provide clear change history for audit and learning

### Typical Communication
- Change request documentation and impact assessments
- Change advisory board meetings
- Change approval notifications
- Post-implementation change reviews

---

## Persona Interactions and Collaboration

Understanding how these personas collaborate is essential for effective project delivery. Here's how the key interactions work:

### Cross-Role Collaboration Patterns

**Developers ↔ Quality Assurance Lead**
- Developers work with QA Lead to define testing strategies and acceptance criteria
- QA Lead reviews code quality metrics and provides feedback on test coverage
- Joint participation in defect triage and resolution planning

**Product Managers ↔ Stakeholder Liaison**
- Stakeholder Liaison gathers requirements and feedback, which informs Product Manager prioritization
- Product Manager shares roadmap updates through Stakeholder Liaison
- Joint facilitation of stakeholder requirement sessions

**Project Managers ↔ Process Owner**
- Project Managers provide feedback on process effectiveness
- Process Owner helps Project Managers implement and adapt processes
- Collaborate on process improvements based on project retrospectives

**Project Managers ↔ Change Manager**
- Project Managers submit scope or timeline change requests
- Change Manager assesses impact and facilitates approval
- Joint communication of approved changes to the team

**Quality Assurance Lead ↔ Process Owner**
- Process Owner integrates QA checkpoints into process documentation
- QA Lead provides input on quality standards in processes
- Collaborate on quality-related process improvements

**Change Manager ↔ All Roles**
- All roles can submit change requests through Change Manager
- Change Manager ensures all affected roles are notified of changes
- Post-change reviews involve input from all impacted personas
